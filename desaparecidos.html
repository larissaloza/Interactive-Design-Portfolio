- DESAPARECIDOS -

Cover for Website - 2022
Created with Adobe Photoshop and After Effects

This was a web portal created for Conahcyt's national strategic program "Desaparecidos", which offers an open access space for data analysis and visualization of information generated under national programs.
The process began with the search for images of documents, and people related to the search for missing persons in Mexico, each of these images was edited to create a sequence as a collage edited.

The issue of missing persons in Mexico is a severe crisis affecting thousands of families. Disappearances, often linked to organized crime and corruption, highlight the lack of effective governmental response. Families endure immense suffering, often searching for their loved ones themselves. This urgent problem underscores the need for systemic reforms to ensure justice and prevent further disappearances.
