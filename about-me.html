Allow me to introduce myself

My name is Larissa, a.k.a Lagrima Perlada. I'm a Digital Media Artist and Interactive Designer. I hold a bachelor's degree in Design and a postgraduate degree specializing in Multimedia Design from the School of Design at the National Institute of Fine Arts. My primary interest lies in pushing boundaries and achieving an intersection of art and technology. My aim is to create works that captivate viewers, offering immersive and interactive visual experiences.

With a background in dance studies, I am particularly interested in expressing the human body in motion. I find constant inspiration in the beauty of nature, immersing myself in the environment.

Currently, I'm delving into topics such as Artificial Intelligence, body tracking, 3D art and animation, and virtual reality. I work with programs like Houdini, Unreal 5, and my personal favorites, Blender and TouchDesigner. This website was both programmed and designed by me using HTML, CSS, and a touch of Processing.

I thoroughly enjoy collaborating with fellow artists and designers. If you're interested in collaborating or simply wish to leave a comment or request, feel free to contact me at larissaloza@gmail.com

Also, check out my Linktree and make sure to follow me on Instagram where I share my creative process and interactive experiments.
