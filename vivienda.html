- VIVIENDA -

Cover for Website - 2022
Created with Adobe Illustrator and After Effects

This was a web portal created for Conahcyt's national strategic program "Vivienda", which offers an open access space for data analysis and visualization of information generated under national programs.
The process started with the illustration of each element, and then it was animated individually to create the complete animation.

The housing shortage in Mexico affects millions, driven by rapid urbanization, economic inequality, and insufficient government policies. Many families live in overcrowded or substandard conditions. Solving this crisis requires better policies, more investment in affordable housing, and efforts to reduce socioeconomic disparities.
